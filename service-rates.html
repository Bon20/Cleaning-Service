Dusting Service

Dusting is a vital part of keeping your facilities clean and your staff and customers healthy. Dust can induce coughing, eye irritation, sneezing, and even hay fever. Dead skin cells, hair filth, pollen, and other nasty things make up dust. Dust mites can live in dust. Microscopic organisms can pose tremendous difficulties. It's important to regularly dust your facility's surfaces. We dust properly. As part of a bigger cleaning campaign, our crew will damp wipe desks, credenzas, windows, and cupboards. We'll remove dust bunnies, cobwebs, fingerprints, and cup stains so you can focus on other things.
